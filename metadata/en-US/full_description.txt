<b>A simple app which lists a bunch of emoticons (not emojis!).</b>

Features:
<ul>
<li>Browse through a list of emoticons which you can copy to clipboard without using internet or any websites.</li>
<li>Add or modify your own emoticons.</li>
<li>Search using related tags.</li>
<li>Reorder them to your liking</li>
<li>Import and export the database.</li>
</ul>

App is written in Flutter, using Material 3 and supports dark/light mode.
